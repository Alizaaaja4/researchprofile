About Me

Research Focus and Biography

I am a Telecommunication Engineering student at Telkom University with strong research interests in cutting-edge technologies including Cloud Computing, Internet of Things (IoT), and Microservices Architecture. My work focuses on developing innovative solutions that bridge telecommunication systems with modern software development paradigms.

My technical expertise spans across multiple domains of computing and software development, with particular emphasis on creating scalable and efficient systems. I combine theoretical knowledge from telecommunication engineering with practical software development skills to approach problems from interdisciplinary perspectives.
